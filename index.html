What is the Duat?


Welcome to the Duat, a platform dedicated to sharing and promoting fully free and open-source software.


Open-source software is a type of software that allows users to access, modify, and distribute the source code. This means that anyone can use, improve, and share the software without any legal or financial constraints.


Free software, on the other hand, refers to software that can be used, studied, and modified without any financial cost. The term "free" in this context refers to freedom, not price. The Free Software Foundation defines free software as "software that respects users' freedom and community."


At the Duat, we believe that free and open-source software is essential for a truly open and equitable digital society. By providing access to the source code, open-source software empowers users to take control of their technology and to collaborate with others to improve it. And by eliminating financial barriers, free software allows everyone to use and benefit from technology, regardless of their financial situation.


On our platform, you will find a wide variety of free and open-source software, including operating systems, productivity tools, novelty oddities, and more. We provide a curated list of carefully hand-picked software for everybody to access in one central place. Some of this software has been made by us, some of it submitted to the Duat, and some of it we just found cool enough to add here. All of the software on our platform is thoroughly vetted to ensure that it meets our standards for freedom and openness.


We believe that free and open-source software is the key to a brighter future for everyone, and we're committed to doing our part to help spread this message. So join us, and discover the power of free and open-source software for yourself.
