About

Welcome to the Duat, the home of the miz Operating System.


The miz Operating System, or mizOS, is a free and open source operating system designed to provide a user-friendly experience without sacrificing performance or system resources. mizOS achieves this by bringing simplicity and uniformity to otherwise complicated software. mizOS is "centralized" - everything you need is in one place.


mizOS is built on top of Arch Linux, a powerful rolling-release Linux distribution that provides access to all of the latest software, tools, and security. With mizOS, you have complete control over your system, and can customize it to fit your needs. Whether you're a software developer, a gamer, or just someone looking for a new system, mizOS is here for you.


We take pride in the fact that mizOS is completely free and open source. This means that you can use, modify, and distribute the operating system as you see fit, without any restrictions or fees. We believe that software should be accessible to everyone, and that the best way to achieve this is through the power of free and open-source.


At the Duat, you'll find all the information you need to get started with mizOS. We provide detailed documentation to help you install and configure the operating system, as well as guides for using the many features and tools that come with it. We also provide a comprehensive list of official and community-made packages that you can install onto your system.
