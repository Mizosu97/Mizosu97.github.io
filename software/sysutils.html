mgpu

License: GNU GPL v.3

Maintainer: Mizosu
Source: github.com/Mizosu97/mgpu

mgpu is a minimal wrapper for DRI_PRIME that makes managing a multi-GPU machine easier. It lets you force a command/program to run on a specific GPU.
